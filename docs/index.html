Energy & Supply Chain (PIR 3)

The rapid growth of hyperscale data centers faces significant power and cooling constraints. These facilities require substantial energy resources, often relying on local power grids and cooling systems vulnerable to disruptions. The 'Compute Divide,' where the majority of advanced computing resources are concentrated in the US and China, exacerbates these vulnerabilities.
